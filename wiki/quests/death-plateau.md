# Death Plateau

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Difficulty | Novice |
| Length | Medium |
| Quest Points | 1 |

## Requirements

- Ability to fight trolls in combat
- 10 Cooking (for cooked trout)

## Items Needed

- Iron bar
- 10 bread
- 10 cooked trout

## Rewards

- 3,000 Attack XP
- Steel claws
- Ability to craft claws (smithing)

## Walkthrough

1. **Start the quest.** Speak to Denulth at the Imperial Guard camp in Burthorpe. He needs to find a safe route past Death Plateau to counter the troll threat.

2. **Visit Eohric.** Go to the castle in Burthorpe and speak to Eohric for information about the plateau.

3. **Find Harold.** Go to the Toad and Chicken Inn in Burthorpe. Find Harold and gamble with him. Win an IOU from him that contains a safe combination.

4. **Talk to Saba.** Find Saba living in a cave on the mountainside. He is a hermit who may know of an alternate path.

5. **Find Tenzing the Sherpa.** Locate Tenzing, who knows a secret path over the mountain. He will help you, but requires supplies first.

6. **Gather supplies.** Collect the following if you do not already have them:
   - 10 cooked trout
   - 10 bread

7. **Get a certificate from Denulth.** Return to Denulth and obtain an equipment certificate for the blacksmith.

8. **Visit Dunstan the blacksmith.** Give Dunstan the certificate and an iron bar. He will repair a pair of spiked climbing boots for you.

9. **Deliver supplies to Tenzing.** Bring the climbing boots, 10 bread, and 10 cooked trout to Tenzing. In exchange, he gives you a secret map showing a safe route past Death Plateau.

10. **Complete the quest.** Return the secret map to Denulth at the Imperial Guard camp.
