## Walkthrough

### Getting Started

Speak to King Lathas in East Ardougne Castle. He tasks you with traversing the Underground Pass beneath the mountains to reach the lands beyond. Travel to the cave entrance in West Ardougne and meet Koftik, your guide.

### Level 1 - The Upper Pass

1. Enter the cave with Koftik. Use fire arrows on the rope bridge guide rope to cross the chasm.
2. Navigate the 5x5 grid puzzle. Step on the correct path of tiles to avoid falling into the pit below. Failed attempts deal damage.
3. Collect 4 orbs of light scattered throughout the level. Each orb is guarded by traps or obstacles.
4. Deposit all 4 orbs into the furnace to open the gate to the next level.

### Level 2 - The Middle Pass

1. Pick locks on doors to progress through the corridors.
2. Cross narrow ledges over gaps. Higher Agility reduces the chance of falling.
3. Defeat 3 Paladins to collect their colored crests (red, blue, and green).
4. Obtain a unicorn horn from the unicorn pen area.
5. Use the crests and horn on their respective doors and mechanisms to proceed.

### Level 3 - The Lower Pass

1. Navigate agility ledges across a cavern. Failures result in falling and taking damage.
2. Find the witch's house within the pass.
3. Retrieve the witch's cat and return it to her to gain passage further into the dungeon.

### Iban's Tomb

1. Gather 4 ritual items needed to construct Iban's doll:
   - Each item is found in a different area of the tomb complex.
2. Equip Zamorak robes (top and bottom) before proceeding.
3. Combine all 4 ritual items to create the doll of Iban.
4. Activate the doll at the well of Iban to destroy his spirit.
5. Collect the Staff of Iban from the well.
6. Return to King Lathas to complete the quest.
