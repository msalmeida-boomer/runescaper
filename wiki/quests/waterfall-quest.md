# Waterfall Quest

| Detail     | Info         |
|------------|--------------|
| Difficulty | Experienced  |
| Length     | Long         |
| Quest Points | 1 QP      |

## Requirements

- No listed skill requirements
- Ability to survive against aggressive monsters (Fire Giants, Shadow Spiders, Skeletons)

## Items Needed

- Food (recommended for low-level players)
- Rope
- 6 water runes
- 6 air runes
- 6 earth runes

## Rewards

- 1 Quest Point
- 13,750 Attack XP
- 13,750 Strength XP
- 2 cut diamonds
- 2 gold bars
- 40 mithril seeds

## Walkthrough

1. Speak to **Almera** in her house north of **Baxtorian Falls**. She is worried about her son Hudon who went to the waterfall.
2. Board the **raft** outside Almera's house. After crashing, talk to the **boy** (Hudon) on the island.
3. Use a **rope** on the rock in the river to cross safely. Then use the rope on the dead tree to swing across.
4. Enter the **barrel** to wash downstream and end up near **Hadley's house**.
5. Search the bookcase in Hadley's house and read the **Book on Baxtorian** to learn about the elven king and his treasures.
6. Travel to the **Tree Gnome Village**. Navigate the maze to reach the dungeon cave entrance.
7. Inside the cave, search the **crates** to find a key. Use it to rescue the imprisoned **gnome**.
8. The gnome gives you **Glarial's pebble** as thanks.
9. Go to **Glarial's Tombstone** (west of Baxtorian Falls). Use the pebble on the tombstone to enter. Retrieve **Glarial's amulet** and **Glarial's urn** from inside. You cannot bring weapons, armour, or runes into the tomb.
10. Return to the **waterfall**. Equip **Glarial's amulet** and board the raft again. Use the rope on the rock, then enter the waterfall door (the amulet grants access).
11. Inside the waterfall dungeon, search a crate to find a **key**.
12. Navigate past the **Fire Giants** to reach the main chamber.
13. Place **6 air runes**, **6 water runes**, and **6 earth runes** on the **6 pillars** in the chamber.
14. Use **Glarial's amulet** on the **statue of Glarial**.
15. Use **Glarial's urn** on the **Chalice of Eternity** (trophy) to complete the quest.
