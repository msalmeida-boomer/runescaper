# Clock Tower

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Difficulty | Intermediate |
| Length | Medium |
| Quest Points | 1 |

## Requirements

- Ability to get past level 48 ogres
- Ice gloves (optional, for handling the black cog through fire)

## Items Needed

- Bucket of water (obtainable during quest)
- Ice gloves (optional but helpful for the black cog)

## Rewards

- 500 gp

## Walkthrough

1. **Start the quest.** Speak to Brother Kojo at the clock tower, located SW of the Ardougne Zoo. The clock has broken down and he needs you to retrieve 4 colored cogs from the dungeon beneath the tower.

2. **Enter the dungeon.** Climb down the ladder in the clock tower to access the dungeon. Colored floor bricks indicate the paths to each cog. You can only carry one cog at a time.

3. **Retrieve the Red Cog.** Follow the red brick path. You will need to get past level 48 ogres. Bring the red cog back up and place it on the **1st floor (1F) pole**.

4. **Retrieve the White Cog.** Follow the white brick path through an area with poison rats. Bring the white cog back up and place it on the **3rd floor (3F) pole**.

5. **Retrieve the Blue Cog.** Follow the blue brick path. You will need to use water at a certain point to proceed. Bring the blue cog back up and place it on the **2nd floor (2F) pole**.

6. **Retrieve the Black Cog.** Follow the black brick path through a section with fire. Use a bucket of water or wear ice gloves to safely handle the cog. Bring the black cog back and place it on the **basement pole**.

7. **Complete the quest.** Once all 4 cogs are placed on their correct poles, return to Brother Kojo to finish the quest.

**Important:** You can only carry one cog at a time. Plan your trips accordingly.
