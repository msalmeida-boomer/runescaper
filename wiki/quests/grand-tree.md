# The Grand Tree

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Difficulty | Experienced |
| Length | Long |
| Quest Points | 5 |

## Requirements

- 25 Agility
- Ability to defeat a Black Demon (level 172)
- Recommended: Tree Gnome Village quest complete

## Items Needed

- None required (all items obtained during quest)

## Rewards

- 11,200 Agility XP
- 18,400 Attack XP
- 2,150 Magic XP
- Access to spirit tree teleportation network
- Access to gnome glider transport system

## Walkthrough

1. **Start the quest.** Speak to King Narnode Shareen on the ground floor of the Grand Tree in the Tree Gnome Stronghold. He tells you the Grand Tree is dying and asks for your help.

2. **Get the translation book.** The King gives you a bark sample. Obtain the translation book and take the bark to Hazelmere, who lives on an island south of Ardougne.

3. **Return to the King.** Bring the translation back to King Narnode. He will be troubled by the message.

4. **Investigate Glough.** Talk to Glough, the King's head tree guardian, who lives in a tree house. Then speak to Charlie, who is being held prisoner.

5. **Find the journal.** Search the cupboard in Glough's house to find his journal, which reveals his plans.

6. **Get arrested.** You will be captured and imprisoned. While in custody, learn the password: **"ka lu min"**.

7. **Travel to Karamja shipyard.** Use the gnome glider to fly to the Karamja shipyard. Speak to the foreman. When questioned, give these answers:
   - His wife is **deceased**
   - Favourite drink: **wormhole**
   - Girlfriend's name: **Anita**

8. **Get the lumber order.** Obtain the foreman's note detailing Glough's order for a fleet of warships. Return to the Stronghold.

9. **Visit Anita.** Speak to Anita to obtain the key to Glough's chest.

10. **Find evidence.** Use the key on Glough's chest to find incriminating evidence of his plans.

11. **The stick puzzle.** Place the letter sticks on the stand in the order: **T - U - Z - O**.

12. **Fight the Black Demon.** Descend into the roots of the Grand Tree and fight the Black Demon (level 172). You can safespot it using the rocks in the cavern.

13. **Complete the quest.** Find the Daconia stone among the roots and return it to King Narnode.
