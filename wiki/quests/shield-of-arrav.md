# Shield of Arrav

| Detail     | Info       |
|------------|------------|
| Difficulty | Novice     |
| Length     | Medium     |
| Quest Points | 1       |

## Requirements

- Another player to complete the quest (each player must join a different gang)

## Items Needed

None (items are obtained during the quest).

## Rewards

- 600 gp

## Walkthrough

### Starting the Quest

1. Speak to **Reldo** in the **Varrock Palace Library**. He directs you to read a book about the Shield of Arrav. The book reveals that the shield was stolen by the Phoenix Gang and has since been split in half between them and the Black Arm Gang.

### Choosing a Gang

One player must join the **Phoenix Gang** and the other must join the **Black Arm Gang**. Both halves of the shield are required to complete the quest.

#### Phoenix Gang Route

2. Speak to **Baraek** the fur trader in Varrock Square. Pay him for the location of the Phoenix Gang hideout.
3. Go to the hideout in southeast Varrock. Speak to the **Straven**. He asks you to kill **Jonny the Beard** at the Blue Moon Inn.
4. Kill Jonny the Beard and collect the **Intel report**. Return to Straven for the gang's **weapon store key**.
5. Open the weapon store chest to find the **right half of the Shield of Arrav**.

#### Black Arm Gang Route

2. Speak to **Charlie the Tramp** near the south entrance to Varrock. He tells you about the Black Arm Gang.
3. Go to the gang's hideout in southwest Varrock. Speak to **Katrine**. She asks you to bring two **Phoenix crossbows** from the Phoenix Gang weapon store.
4. Have your Phoenix Gang partner retrieve the crossbows from the weapon store. Trade them to you.
5. Give the crossbows to Katrine for access. Search the cupboard upstairs for the **left half of the Shield of Arrav**.

### Completing the Quest

6. Trade shield halves between the two players so each has one half.
7. Take both halves to the **Varrock Museum** curator, **Haig Halen**. He combines them and gives each player a **certificate half**.
8. Trade certificate halves so each player has a complete certificate.
9. Bring the complete certificate to **King Roald** in Varrock Palace to claim the reward.
