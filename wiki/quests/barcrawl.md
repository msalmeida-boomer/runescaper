# Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl

## Overview

| Detail       | Info                  |
|--------------|-----------------------|
| Difficulty   | Easy                  |
| Length       | Medium                |
| Quest Points | 0 (mini-quest)        |

## Requirements

No skill or quest requirements.

## Items Needed

- Barcrawl card (given at start)
- Coins (enough to buy a drink at each bar)

## Rewards

- Access to the Barbarian Outpost agility course

## Walkthrough

### Getting Started

Speak to the Barbarian guard at the Barbarian Outpost gate, located northeast of Baxtorian Falls. He challenges you to complete Alfred Grimhand's Barcrawl before he will grant you entry.

You receive a Barcrawl card that must be stamped at each bar.

### Bar Locations

Visit the following bars in any order. Buy and drink the specialty drink at each one to get your card stamped:

| Bar                          | Location                          |
|------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Jolly Boar Inn               | Northeast of Varrock              |
| Blue Moon Inn                | Varrock (south side)              |
| Rising Sun Inn               | Falador                           |
| Rusty Anchor Inn             | Port Sarim                        |
| Dead Man's Chest             | Brimhaven                         |
| Karamja Spirits Bar          | Karamja (Musa Point)              |
| Dragon Inn                   | Yanille                           |
| Flying Horse Inn             | Ardougne                          |
| Forester's Arms              | Seers' Village                    |
| Blurberry's Bar              | Tree Gnome Stronghold, Grand Tree 2F |

Each drink has a different effect on your character (stat reductions, staggering, etc.). The effects are temporary.

### Completing the Barcrawl

Once all bars have been visited and your card is fully stamped, return to the Barbarian guard at the Barbarian Outpost gate. Present your completed Barcrawl card to gain access to the agility course.

## Key NPCs & Locations

| NPC/Location            | Role                                       |
|-------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| Barbarian guard         | Quest start, Barbarian Outpost gate        |
| Bartenders (10 bars)    | Stamp your Barcrawl card                   |
| Barbarian Outpost       | NE of Baxtorian Falls, reward location     |
