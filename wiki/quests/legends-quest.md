## Walkthrough

### Phase 1 - Mapping the Jungle

1. Speak to Radimus Erkle at the Legends' Guild entrance to begin the quest. He gives you a mapping assignment.
2. Travel to the Kharazi Jungle south of Shilo Village.
3. Use papyrus and charcoal to map 3 distinct areas of the jungle (west, middle, east sections).
4. Complete the map by visiting all 3 regions.

### Phase 2 - Contacting Gujuo

1. Craft a bull roarer (use a knife on achey logs, attach rope).
2. Swing the bull roarer in the jungle to summon the native Gujuo.
3. Speak with Gujuo to learn about the troubles plaguing the jungle and the totem poles.

### Phase 3 - The Golden Bowl

1. Craft a golden bowl using 2 gold bars on an anvil (requires the golden bowl sketch from Gujuo).
2. Have the golden bowl blessed by Gujuo.
3. Use the blessed bowl to collect holy water from the sacred pool.
4. Use the holy water to rescue Ungadulu, who is trapped behind a fire wall in the cave.

### Phase 4 - Dungeon Puzzles

1. Enter the dungeon beneath the jungle.
2. Navigate gate puzzles by pulling levers in the correct sequence.
3. Push boulders to clear passages.
4. Solve the riddle door by using runes that spell **SMELL** (Soul, Mind, Earth, Law, Law).
5. Place gems (sapphire, emerald, ruby, diamond) in the correct receptacles to open the final door.

### Phase 5 - Demon Fight (First Encounter)

1. Confront the level 187 demon Nezikchened in the deeper cavern.
2. Defeat it in combat. Prayer and high-level combat gear are strongly recommended.

### Phase 6 - Bravery Potion

1. Gather ingredients for the Bravery Potion (snake weed and ardrigal).
2. Brew the potion and administer it to Ungadulu to fully restore him.

### Phase 7 - Deep Cave

1. Descend into the deepest section of the cave.
2. Fight 3 warrior spirits in succession. Each must be defeated to proceed.
3. Collect crystal pieces from each warrior spirit.
4. Combine the 3 crystal pieces into a single heart crystal.

### Phase 8 - Demon Rematches

1. Nezikchened returns for multiple additional fights throughout the quest.
2. Be prepared with food, prayer potions, and combat equipment for each encounter.

### Phase 9 - Yommi Tree

1. Obtain Yommi tree seeds from Gujuo.
2. Plant the seeds in fertile soil within the jungle.
3. Use holy water to nurture the tree as it grows.
4. Chop the grown Yommi tree to carve it into a totem pole.

### Phase 10 - Replacing the Totems

1. Take the carved totem pole to the dark totem pole locations in the jungle.
2. Replace the corrupted Dark Totem Poles with your gilded totem.
3. The demon Nezikchened may appear for a final confrontation during this step.

### Completing the Quest

Return the Gilded Totem to Radimus Erkle at the Legends' Guild to complete the quest and gain access to the Guild.
