# Watch Tower

## Overview

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Difficulty | Experienced |
| Length | Long |
| Quest Points | 4 |

## Requirements

- 14 Magic
- 14 Herblore
- 15 Thieving
- 25 Agility
- 40 Mining

## Items Needed

- Lit candle
- Bat bones
- 2 ropes
- Jangerberry
- Guam leaf
- Pestle and mortar
- Death rune
- Combat gear and food

## Rewards

- 4 Quest Points
- 12,000 Magic XP
- 5,000 gp
- Watchtower Teleport spell (requires 58 Magic)

## Walkthrough

### Getting Started

1. Climb the rocks to reach the **Watchtower** north of Yanille.
2. Speak to the watchtower wizard. The ogres are planning an invasion and the tower's magic defences are failing.
3. Search the **bushes** outside the watchtower for clues about the ogre threat.

### The Ogre Settlements

4. Travel to the ogre settlement to the west. Meet **Og**, one of the ogre chieftains.
5. Use a rope to **swing to the island** where a jangerberry bush grows (or bring your own jangerberries).
6. Fight and defeat **Gorad** (level 56), another ogre chieftain.
7. Obtain a **gold bar** from **Toban**, the third chieftain, through dialogue.
8. **Assemble the statue** using the gold bar and other components to gain access to the Ogre city.

### Inside the Ogre City

9. Enter the **Ogre city** (Gu'Tanoth).
10. **Steal a rock cake** from an ogre stall using your Thieving skill.
11. Navigate the city's various puzzles and obstacles using agility and items.

### The Scavid Caves

12. Learn the **Scavid language** by speaking with various Scavid creatures in the caves beneath the city.
13. Use the correct Scavid phrases to progress through locked doors and collect **crystals** needed for the watchtower.

### Brewing the Enchanted Potion

14. Brew the **enchanted potion** by combining:
    - Guam leaf
    - Jangerberry
    - Ground bat bones (use pestle and mortar on bat bones)
15. Use the potion on the watchtower's power source.

### Eliminating the Shamans

16. Track down and eliminate the **ogre shamans** who are behind the magical assault on the watchtower.
17. **Mine the final crystal** using your Mining skill (40 Mining required).

### Completion

18. Place all crystals on the watchtower pillars to restore the tower's magical defences.
19. Speak to the watchtower wizard to complete the quest and unlock the **Watchtower Teleport** spell.
