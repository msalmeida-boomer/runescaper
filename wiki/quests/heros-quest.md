# Hero's Quest

## Overview

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Difficulty | Experienced |
| Length | Long |
| Quest Points | 1 |

## Requirements

- Completion of **Shield of Arrav**, **Lost City**, **Merlin's Crystal**, and **Dragon Slayer**
- 55 Quest Points
- 50 Mining
- 25 Herblore
- 53 Fishing
- 53 Cooking

## Items Needed

- Ice Gloves (obtained during quest)
- Oily Fishing Rod (obtained during quest)
- Fishing bait
- Harralander potion (unf)
- Blamish snail slime
- Antifire shield or dragonfire shield
- Combat gear
- Food

## Rewards

- 1 Quest Point
- 3,075 XP each in Attack, Defence, Strength, and Hitpoints
- XP in Ranged, Fishing, Cooking, Woodcutting, Firemaking, Smithing, Mining, and Herblore
- Access to the Hero's Guild
- Ability to purchase Dragon Axe and Dragon Mace

## Walkthrough

Speak to **Achetties** at the Hero's Guild north of Taverley. She requires you to bring three items to prove your worth.

### Item 1: Firebird Feather

1. Travel to **White Wolf Mountain** and descend into the ice cavern.
2. Navigate through the icy tunnels to find and kill the **Ice Queen**.
3. She drops **Ice Gloves**. Keep these equipped.
4. Travel to **Entrana** (no weapons or armour allowed on the boat, but Ice Gloves are permitted).
5. Find and kill the **Firebird** on Entrana.
6. Pick up the **Firebird Feather** while wearing Ice Gloves (it will burn you without them).

### Item 2: Master Thieves Armband

This item requires cooperation between members of the **Black Arm Gang** and **Phoenix Gang** (from Shield of Arrav). You need a partner from the opposite gang.

7. Travel to **Brimhaven** and locate **ScarFace Pete's mansion**.
8. **Black Arm member**: Enter through the back entrance and deal with guards inside. Obtain the candlesticks.
9. **Phoenix Gang member**: Obtain the key to the chest containing the armband.
10. Through cooperation, obtain the **Master Thieves Armband**.

### Item 3: Cooked Lava Eel

11. Speak to **Gerrant** in Port Sarim's fishing shop. He helps you create an **Oily Fishing Rod** using blamish oil (made from Harralander potion and blamish snail slime).
12. Travel to **Taverley Dungeon** and navigate past the **Blue Dragons** (bring antifire protection).
13. Fish at the lava stream using the Oily Fishing Rod and bait to catch a **Lava Eel**.
14. Cook the lava eel to obtain a **Cooked Lava Eel**.

### Completion

15. Return all three items to **Achetties** at the Hero's Guild to complete the quest.
