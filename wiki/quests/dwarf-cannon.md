# Dwarf Cannon

## Overview

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Difficulty | Novice |
| Length | Short |
| Quest Points | 1 |

## Requirements

- No mandatory skill requirements

### Recommended

- 20 Agility (for shortcuts)

## Items Needed

- None required (all items obtained during quest)

## Rewards

- 1 Quest Point
- 750 Crafting XP
- Ability to purchase the Dwarf Multicannon (750,000 gp)
- Ability to craft cannonballs
- Ammo mould

## Walkthrough

1. Speak to the **Dwarf Commander** across the log bridge from Seers' Village to start the quest.
2. He asks you to help repair the perimeter fence that goblins have been attacking.
3. Repair all **6 fence sections** around the dwarf camp. Click on each broken railing to fix it.
4. Report back to the commander. He asks you to investigate the watchtower.
5. Investigate the **watchtower** and find **dwarf remains** inside, evidence of a goblin attack.
6. Report back to the commander. He tells you that **Gilob** has been captured.
7. Rescue **Gilob** from the cave near the windmill southeast of the Fishing Guild.
8. Return to the dwarf camp. The commander asks you to fix the broken cannon.
9. Repair the **cannon** in the shed by using the toolkit on it.
10. The commander needs cannonball ammunition. Travel to **Nulodion** at South Ice Mountain.
11. Speak to Nulodion to receive the **ammo mould** and instructions for crafting cannonballs.
12. Return to the commander to complete the quest.
