# Elemental Workshop

## Overview

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Difficulty | Novice |
| Length | Short |
| Quest Points | 1 |

## Requirements

- 20 Mining
- 20 Smithing
- 20 Crafting
- Ability to defeat a level 35 Earth Elemental

## Items Needed

- Knife
- Leather
- Needle
- Thread
- 4 coal

## Rewards

- 1 Quest Point
- 5,000 Crafting XP
- 5,000 Smithing XP
- Elemental Shield
- Access to the Elemental Workshop

## Walkthrough

1. Search the **bookcase** in the house southwest of Seers' Village bank to find the **Battered Book**.
2. Read the Battered Book to learn about the Elemental Workshop.
3. Use a **knife** on the book to cut out a **battered key**.
4. Use the key on the **Odd Looking Wall** to enter the Elemental Workshop.
5. Go down the stairs into the workshop.
6. Mine the **elemental rock** to obtain elemental ore. A level 35 **Earth Elemental** will appear -- defeat it.
7. Operate the **valves** and **lever** to get the waterwheel running and power the workshop machinery.
8. Repair the **bellows** using leather, needle, and thread.
9. Fill the **stone bowl** with lava by operating the bellows to pump air into the furnace.
10. Smelt the **elemental ore** with **4 coal** at the workshop furnace to create an elemental metal bar.
11. Use the elemental metal bar on the **workbench** to craft the **Elemental Shield**.
