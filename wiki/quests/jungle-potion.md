# Jungle Potion

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Difficulty | Intermediate |
| Length | Short |
| Quest Points | 1 |

## Requirements

- Druidic Ritual quest complete

## Items Needed

- 432 gp (to buy antipoison from Jiminua's store, recommended)

## Rewards

- 775 Herblore XP

## Walkthrough

1. **Start the quest.** Speak to Trufitus in the village of Tai Bwo Wannai on Karamja. He needs 5 jungle herbs to commune with the gods.

2. **Buy antipoison.** Before venturing into the jungle, purchase antipoison from Jiminua's store nearby for 432 gp. The jungle contains poisonous creatures.

3. **Collect Snake Weed.** Head SW of the village to the marshy jungle area. Search the vines to find Snake Weed.

4. **Collect Ardrigal.** Travel NE of the village to a cluster of palm trees. Search them to find Ardrigal.

5. **Collect Sito Foil.** Go south of the village to an area of scorched earth near a fire-damaged building. Search the ground to find Sito Foil.

6. **Collect Vocenia Moss.** Head SE of the village to find a rock pile. Search it to collect Vocenia Moss.

7. **Collect Rogue's Purse.** Enter the dungeon SE of the village. Navigate past the Jogres inside. Search the fungus on the dungeon wall to find Rogue's Purse.

8. **Complete the quest.** Return all 5 herbs to Trufitus in Tai Bwo Wannai.
