# Mage Arena

## Overview

| Detail       | Info                  |
|--------------|-----------------------|
| Difficulty   | Hard                  |
| Length       | Short                 |
| Quest Points | 0 (mini-quest)        |

## Requirements

### Skill Requirements

- 60 Magic

## Items Needed

- Knife or slashing weapon (to cut webs)
- Runes for combat spells
- 80,000 gp (to purchase a God Staff)
- Food and armor

## Rewards

- God Cape (Zamorak, Saradomin, or Guthix)
- God Staff (80,000 gp from shop)
- God spell (unlocked after 100 casts in the arena)

## Walkthrough

### Getting There

1. The Mage Arena is located in level 55 Wilderness, west of the members gate. **Warning: This is deep Wilderness and you risk losing items to PKers.**
2. Pull the lever located west of Ardougne Palace to teleport into the Wilderness near the arena.
3. Enter the building. Cut through the spider webs with a knife or slashing weapon.
4. Pull the lever inside to enter the arena area.

### Fighting Kolodion

1. Speak to Kolodion inside the arena. You must have 60 Magic to proceed.
2. Fight Kolodion through 4 progressively stronger forms. He transforms after each defeat.
3. Use combat magic throughout the fight. Bring food and be prepared for a sustained battle.

### Choosing Your God

1. After defeating all 4 forms, step into the sparkling pool.
2. You are transported to a chamber with 3 god statues. Choose one god:

| God        | Spell Effect                                      |
|------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| Zamorak    | Flames of Zamorak - highest damage, reduces target's Magic level |
| Saradomin  | Saradomin Strike - reduces target's Prayer level  |
| Guthix     | Claws of Guthix - reduces target's Defence level  |

3. Pray at your chosen god's statue to receive the corresponding God Cape.

### Obtaining the God Staff

1. Purchase a God Staff from the staff shop near Kolodion for 80,000 gp.
2. The staff corresponds to the god you chose.

### Unlocking the God Spell for Outside Use

1. The god spell is initially restricted to use only inside the Mage Arena.
2. Cast your god spell 100 times while inside the arena to unlock it for use anywhere.
3. After 100 casts, the spell can be used freely outside the arena with any magic staff.
