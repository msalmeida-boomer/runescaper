# Fight Arena

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Difficulty | Experienced |
| Length | Medium |
| Quest Points | 2 |

## Requirements

- Ability to defeat enemies of levels 44, 55, and 132 (all safespottable)

## Items Needed

- None required (all items obtained during quest)

## Rewards

- 3,375 Thieving XP
- 12,175 Attack XP
- 1,000 gp

## Walkthrough

1. **Start the quest.** Speak to Lady Servil, found SE of the Gnome Battlefield. Her family has been captured by General Khazard and forced to fight in his arena.

2. **Obtain Khazard armour.** Go to the NE house near the Fight Arena and search the chest to find a set of Khazard armour. Equip it to disguise yourself.

3. **Find Jeremy.** Talk to Jeremy (Lady Servil's son) in the prison cells near the arena.

4. **Get the Khali brew.** Go to the tavern and obtain a Khali brew. Give it to the lazy guard outside the prison to make him drowsy.

5. **Free Jeremy.** Take the keys from the guard and use them on Jeremy's prison door to release him.

6. **Fight in the arena.** You will be forced to fight three opponents in succession:
   - **Khazard Ogre** (level 55)
   - **Khazard Scorpion** (level 44)
   - **Bouncer** (level 132) - General Khazard's pet dog

   All three can be safespotted using the arena terrain.

7. **Speak to Jeremy.** After surviving the fights, talk to Jeremy.

8. **Complete the quest.** Report back to Lady Servil SE of the battlefield to claim your reward.
