# Digsite Quest

## Overview

| Detail       | Info    |
|--------------|---------|
| Difficulty   | Novice  |
| Length       | Long    |

## Requirements

### Skill Requirements

- 25 Thieving
- 10 Agility
- 10 Herblore

## Items Needed

- Cup of tea (for an examiner)
- Pestle and mortar
- Vial
- Tinderbox

## Rewards

- 15,300 Mining XP
- 2,000 Herblore XP
- 2 Gold bars

## Walkthrough

### Getting Started

Speak to one of the Examiners at the Digsite Examination Centre, located southeast of Varrock. They will ask you to get your certificate stamped at the Varrock Museum before you can participate in the dig.

### Passing the Exams

1. Travel to the Varrock Museum and get your certificate stamped.
2. Return to the Digsite and pass 3 progressive exams.
3. Consult the students wearing colored shirts scattered around the Digsite for the correct exam answers. Each student corresponds to one exam level.
4. Answer each exam correctly to advance your certification level.

### Collecting Samples

1. Collect rock samples from various sources:
   - Search bushes around the Digsite.
   - Pan for specimens at the panning area.
   - Pickpocket workmen around the Digsite (requires 25 Thieving).
2. Obtain a trowel from one of the Digsite workers.
3. Use the trowel to excavate at dig spots to uncover the Zaros Talisman.

### The Discovery

1. Enter the mine shaft at the Digsite.
2. Collect an Arcenia root from inside the shaft.
3. Collect and identify ammonium nitrate from the specimen trays.
4. Obtain nitroglycerin (found in a barrel within the Digsite).
5. Combine the ammonium nitrate, nitroglycerin, Arcenia root, and a tinderbox to create a chemical compound.
6. Use the compound on the brick barrier blocking the deeper excavation area to destroy it.
7. Retrieve the stone tablet from beyond the barrier.
8. Return the stone tablet to the Examiners to complete the quest.
