# Nature Spirit

| Detail     | Info     |
|------------|----------|
| Difficulty | Novice   |
| Length     | Medium   |
| Quest Points | 2 QP  |

## Requirements

- Completion of Priest in Peril
- Completion of The Restless Ghost
- 18 Crafting
- Ability to defeat a level 30 monster

## Items Needed

- Ghostspeak amulet
- Silver bar
- Silver sickle mould (purchasable from Al Kharid crafting shop)

## Rewards

- 2 Quest Points
- 2,000 Defence XP
- 2,000 Hitpoints XP
- 3,000 Crafting XP
- Blessed Silver Sickle
- Druid Pouch

## Walkthrough

1. Speak to **Drezel** in the **Underground Saradomin Temple** beneath the temple east of Varrock.
2. Pass through the **Morytania portal** and navigate the swamp south toward the circular island.
3. On the island, collect the **washing bowl** and **mirror** from the ground.
4. Search the **grotto** entrance to find Filliman's **journal**.
5. Equip the **Ghostspeak amulet** and use the mirror on **Filliman Tarlock**. He realises he is a ghost.
6. Give Filliman his journal. He reads it and provides you with a **druid spell**.
7. Return to **Drezel** to receive a blessing needed for the ritual.
8. Cast the druid spell on the **rotten logs** near the grotto to produce fungi.
9. Place the required items on the **stones** around the grotto as part of the ritual.
10. Enter the **grotto** to meet the Nature Spirit.
11. If you do not already have one, buy a **silver sickle mould** from the crafting shop in **Al Kharid**.
12. Use the silver bar with a furnace to smelt a **silver sickle**.
13. Dip the silver sickle in the grotto pool to create a **Blessed Silver Sickle**.
14. Use the Blessed Silver Sickle on rotting logs to gather **mort myre fungi** and fill the **Druid Pouch**.
15. Defeat **3 Ghasts** in the Mort Myre Swamp using the filled Druid Pouch to complete the quest.
