# Hitpoints

Hitpoints determine how much damage you can take before dying. All players start with 10 Hitpoints.

## Training

Hitpoints XP is gained passively during all combat. For every point of damage dealt, you receive **1.33 Hitpoints XP** regardless of combat style.

## Death

When hitpoints reach 0, you die and respawn in Lumbridge. You lose all items except your 3 most valuable (or 4 with Protect Item prayer).

## Recovery

| Method | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Food | Restores varying HP depending on food type |
| Rapid Heal prayer (level 22) | Doubles natural HP regeneration rate |
| Monks at monastery | Heal you for free |

## Poison

Some monsters and weapons inflict poison, causing gradual HP loss over time.

| Cure | Source |
|------|--------|
| Antipoison potion | Herblore (level 5) or shops |
| Superantipoison | Herblore (level 48) |
| Curing prayers | Prayer book |
